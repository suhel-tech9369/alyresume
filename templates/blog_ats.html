ATS Resume Kaise Banaye (2026 Guide)

Aaj kal companies resume manually nahi dekhti — wo ATS (Applicant Tracking System) ka use karti hain.

Agar aapka resume ATS friendly nahi hai, to wo direct reject ho sakta hai.

---

📄 Ek acha resume me kya hona chahiye?

Ek professional resume me ye sab hona zaroori hai:

  • Full Name aur Contact Details
  • Professional Summary
  • Skills (relevant keywords)
  • Education
  • Experience (agar hai)
  • Projects aur Certifications

👉 Sab kuch clean aur structured format me hona chahiye

---

❌ Common mistakes jo log karte hain

  • Fancy design use karna
  • Keywords missing hona
  • Long paragraphs likhna
  • Irrelevant information add karna
---

🎯 ATS Resume kya hota hai?

ATS resume wo hota hai jo machine easily read kar sake.

Isme:

  • Simple format hota hai
  • Right keywords hote hain
  • Clear headings hoti hain
---

⚡ ATS Resume kaise banaye?

  • Simple template use karo
  • Job related keywords add karo
  • Clean formatting rakho
  • Bullet points use karo
---
