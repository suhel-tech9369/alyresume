ATS Score Kya Hota Hai? (2026 Guide)

Aaj kal companies resume manually nahi dekhti. 👉 Wo ATS (Applicant Tracking System) ka use karti hain.

📊 ATS Score kya hota hai?

ATS score ek number hota hai jo batata hai ki aapka resume job ke liye kitna fit hai.

  • Keywords match ho rahe hain ya nahi
  • Skills relevant hain ya nahi
  • Formatting sahi hai ya nahi

❌ Problem kya hoti hai?

Most log resume bana dete hain lekin ATS ke liye optimize nahi karte. 👉 Isliye resume reject ho jata hai.

🔥 ATS Score kaise check kare?

  • Apna resume upload karo
  • System aapko score dega (out of 100)
  • Weakness batayega
  • Improve karne ke tips dega

🚀 Smart Tool (AlyResume)

Is website me aap:

  • Resume upload karte ho

  • Missing keywords pata chalte hain
  • Improve karne ke suggestions milte hain

📈 ATS Score kaise improve kare?

  • Job related keywords use karo
  • Simple format rakho
  • Bullet points use karo
  • Irrelevant cheeze hatao

📊 Conclusion

Agar aap job ke liye serious hain, to ATS score check karna zaroori hai.
