Interview Me Kya Puchte Hain (2026 Guide)

Agar aap job ke liye prepare kar rahe hain, to sabse bada sawal hota hai: 👉 interview me kya puchte hain?

🎤 Interview me kya pucha jata hai?

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why should we hire you?
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Why do you want this job?
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

📄 Resume ke basis par questions

Sabse important baat: 👉 Interview me sabse zyada questions aapke resume se hi puchte hain.

  • Aapne jo project likha hai wo explain karo
  • Aapne ye skill kaise seekhi?
  • Aapka experience kya hai?
  • Aapne ye field kyu choose ki?

❌ Common mistakes

  • Resume me galat information likhna
  • Apne project explain na kar paana
  • Confidence ki kami

🔥 Smart Preparation (Best Tarika)

Aaj ke time me best tarika hai: 👉 Apne resume ke according practice karna

Agar aap random questions practice karte hain to fayda kam hota hai. Lekin agar aapke resume ke hisaab se questions milte hain, to aap interview me strong perform karte hain.
