Cover Letter Kaise Likhe (2026 Guide)

Job apply karte time sirf resume kaafi nahi hota. 👉 Ek strong cover letter bhi zaroori hota hai.

📄 Cover Letter kya hota hai?

Cover letter ek short letter hota hai jisme aap company ko batate hain:

  • Aap kaun hain
  • Aap job ke liye kyu fit hain
  • Aap company me kyu kaam karna chahte hain

❌ Common mistakes

  • Copy paste cover letter use karna
  • Generic likhna
  • Resume repeat kar dena
